# Delete your Perspective account

**App:** Perspective: AI assisted CBT  
**Package:** `com.perspectivecbtapp`  
**Contact:** [support@perspectivecbtapp.com](mailto:support@perspectivecbtapp.com)

You can delete your Perspective account and associated server data at any time.

## Delete in the app (recommended)

1. Open **Perspective** on your device.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Open **Data & export**.
4. Tap **Delete account**.
5. Confirm the deletion.

You need to be signed in and online. This permanently deletes your account and synced server data (including synced inbox, vault, profile fields, and usage records tied to your account). Local data on that device is also cleared as part of the flow.

## Request deletion by email

If you cannot use the in-app option, email us from the address on your account:

**[support@perspectivecbtapp.com](mailto:support@perspectivecbtapp.com?subject=Delete%20my%20Perspective%20account)**

Please include:

- The subject line: **Delete my Perspective account**
- The email address used for your Perspective account

We will verify the request and delete associated account data from our production systems. We aim to complete deletion requests within a reasonable time (typically within 30 days), subject to limited exceptions such as short-lived backups, legal obligations, or security logs.

## What is deleted

When your account is deleted, we remove associated personal data from our production database, including:

- Account and profile information
- Synced inbox thoughts and vault entries
- Preferences and related account records stored on our servers

## What is not undone by account deletion

- **Google Play subscriptions** are managed separately in Google Play. Deleting your Perspective account does **not** automatically cancel a Play subscription. Manage or cancel billing in [Google Play subscriptions](https://play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions?package=com.perspectivecbtapp).
- Data stored only on another device may remain until you clear local data or uninstall the app on that device.
